White Water Rafting

Our Purpose:

To provide thrilling and unforgettable white water rafting experiences that connect people with nature, promote adventure, and create lasting memories.

Our Mission:

We are dedicated to offering safe and exhilarating rafting adventures for adventurers of all levels. Our mission is to foster a deep appreciation for our natural environment, promote outdoor recreation, and ensure the well-being of our guests and staff.

Our Creed:

At Redhawk Rapids, we live by the creed of "Adventure, Unity, Conservation." We believe in embracing the spirit of adventure, fostering a sense of unity among our guests and team, and contributing to the conservation of the beautiful rivers and landscapes we explore.

Our Motto:

"Adventure Meets Tradition, Where Rapids Roar and Nature Soars!"

History

Founded by Native American Aiyana Redhawk in the early 1900s, "Redhawk Rapids" began as a Grand Canyon white water rafting company. Aiyana's deep connection to the land led to thrilling river expeditions. His commitment to environmental preservation set Redhawk Rapids apart. The company grew, becoming an icon of responsible adventure tourism. Aiyana's daughter, Sakari, continued his legacy, expanding operations across the American West. Today, Redhawk Rapids remains a symbol of Native American heritage and sustainable adventure, offering unforgettable journeys while safeguarding the natural beauty of the rivers and canyons that have been its home for over a century.
